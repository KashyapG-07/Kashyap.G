How to Play

Guess the Wordle in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

-If the letter entered is Green, then it is in the right spot.
-If the letter entered is Yellow, then it is in the word but in the wrong spot.
-If the letter entered is Grey, then it is not in the word in any spot.

Restart:

- At any point, you can start a new game by refreshing the page.
